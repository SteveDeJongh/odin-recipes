Pancakes

The Allans famous pancake recipe

A classic in the Allan household for mid week dinners, batches are often doubled for to ensure some get left for lunch!

Each batch makes around 8 pancakes.

Ingredients

  • White Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Optional fillings

Directons

  1. Mix together ingredients until just combined.
  2. Warm pan to medium heat.
  3. Flip once top side begins to bubble.
